Pando: The Trembling Giant

Science Conservation Visit

Earth's Largest Living Organism

Spanning 106 acres and weighing 6,600 tons, Pando is a single clonal colony of quaking aspen that has lived for over 80,000 years in Utah.

Quick Facts

  • Location: Fishlake National Forest, Utah
  • Age: 80,000+ years
  • Weight: 6,600 tons
  • Area: 106 acres
  • Number of stems: ~47,000
